## Brexit changed the rules in your favor

Before 1 January 2021, UK residents were part of the EU. No VAT refund on French purchases.

Since Brexit, the UK sits outside the EU. French law treats UK residents as non-EU travelers. The 20% VAT you pay on purchases in France can be refunded.

## Who qualifies

- **You live in the United Kingdom.** England, Scotland, Wales, Northern Ireland. A British passport or proof of UK residency works.
- **You're buying for personal use.** Not for resale.
- **You spend at least €100.01 (TTC)** in a single visit to one store (or combined across Belcova partner stores).
- **You leave the EU within 3 months** of purchase with the goods.

### Northern Ireland

Northern Ireland has a special status under the Brexit protocol. For goods, it stays aligned with EU rules. Northern Ireland residents qualify for détaxe in France because the UK as a whole is outside the EU.

## How much you get back

Standard French VAT is 20%. The refund operator takes a cut.

| Purchase | VAT included | Refund (traditional operator, 55%) | Refund (Belcova, 80%) |
|----------|-------------|-------------------------------------|------------------------|
| £200 (~€235) | ~€39 | ~€21 | ~€31 |
| £500 (~€590) | ~€98 | ~€54 | ~€79 |
| £1,000 (~€1,175) | ~€196 | ~€108 | ~€157 |

A weekend shopping trip to Paris with £1,000 in purchases gets you €108 to €157 back, depending on the operator.

## The process

### 1. At the store

Show your British passport at checkout. The retailer creates a digital tax refund form. Check that the passport number is correct before you leave the store.

With Belcova, you can shop at any store. Ask for a receipt, photograph it in the app.

### 2. Before leaving the EU

**Eurostar (Gare du Nord):** PABLO kiosks sit in the departure control area. Scan your form before crossing to the UK side. Hours: 06:00 to 21:00.

**By air (CDG, Orly):** scan at a PABLO kiosk in the departure terminal. See our [CDG PABLO kiosk guide](/en/blog/pablo-kiosk-cdg-guide) for terminal-by-terminal locations.

**By ferry (Calais, Dunkirk):** PABLO kiosks are available at the ferry terminal. Scan before boarding.

**Channel Tunnel (Eurotunnel):** kiosks are available at the Calais/Coquelles terminal.

### 3. Get your refund

After PABLO validation: 3 to 5 business days (Belcova) or 10 to 15 days (traditional operators). The transfer arrives in euros on your card. Your UK bank converts to pounds at the day's rate.

## Where Brits shop most in France

**Paris.** Weekend trips, same-day Eurostar returns. Galeries Lafayette, Le Bon Marché, luxury boutiques.

**Calais and the Opal Coast.** Groceries, wine, spirits. Cité Europe in Calais is a shopping center 5 minutes from the tunnel. The €100.01 threshold is met in a single supermarket trip.

**Nice and the Côte d'Azur.** Summer holidays, luxury purchases. PABLO kiosks at Nice airport cover return flights to the UK.

**Bordeaux, Lyon, Strasbourg.** Budget airline routes from the UK. Wine, food, fashion.

## What's different for UK tourists

**Nothing legally.** The process is identical for all non-EU residents. Passport, form, PABLO kiosk, refund.

**The practical difference:** many Brits make frequent trips (Eurostar, ferry, tunnel). You can claim on every visit. No frequency limit.

**Currency conversion:** your refund arrives in euros. Conversion fees depend on your bank. Digital banks (Monzo, Revolut, Starling) use the interbank rate with no markup. Traditional banks charge 1.5 to 3%.

## FAQ

**I have an Irish passport but live in England. Am I eligible?**
Yes. Détaxe depends on where you live, not your nationality. If you reside in the UK, you qualify with proof of residency.

**I'm taking the Eurostar. Where and when do I scan?**
At Gare du Nord, in the departure control zone. PABLO kiosks are open 06:00 to 21:00. Allow 10 extra minutes before boarding.

**Are supermarket purchases eligible?**
Yes. Food, wine, pharmacy cosmetics. All physical goods above €100.01 qualify. See our [guide to tax refunds on groceries](/en/blog/tax-refund-groceries-france).

**I'm doing a day trip through the Channel Tunnel. Does it work?**
Yes. Scan at the Calais/Coquelles terminal before entering the tunnel. Your form is valid from the moment it's created.

**Does détaxe apply in Belgium too?**
Yes, but with a different operator and Belgian rules. French détaxe covers purchases in France only. Belgian purchases require a separate claim with a Belgian operator.
